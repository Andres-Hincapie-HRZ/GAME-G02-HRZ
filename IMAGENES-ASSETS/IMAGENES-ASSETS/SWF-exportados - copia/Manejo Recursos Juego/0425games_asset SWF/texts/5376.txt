1.Bind: Select a Bionic Chip and click on the Bind button to set the status of the chip to “Bound”. This prevents it from being converted into experience and consumed during a “Merge All” action.2.Merge All: Select a Bionic Chip you wish to enhance and click the “Merge All” button. This will convert all unbound Bionic Chips into experience and merge them with the selected Bionic Chip.3.Scrap All: Sell all Scrap Bionic Chips for Corsairs’ Gold.4.Generating new Bionic Chips requires Corsairs’ Gold which can be obtained from exploring Humaroids or traded with Commander Cards, Blueprints, Honor, or Badges.
